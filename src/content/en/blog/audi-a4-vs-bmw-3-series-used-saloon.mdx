# Audi A4 vs BMW 3 Series: Which Used Saloon Makes More Sense?

The Audi A4 and BMW 3 Series are close rivals, but they fail in different ways when neglected. The A4 often wins for refinement and secure road feel. The 3 Series usually wins for driving balance and steering feel.

Use the direct comparison page here: [Audi A4 vs BMW 3 Series](/compare/audi-a4-vs-bmw-3-series).

## Audi A4 buying logic

Do not judge an A4 by badge, trim, or quattro branding alone. Confirm the exact engine and gearbox first. EA888 petrol risk changes by generation, 2.0 TDI cars need emissions checks, and S tronic history can decide whether the car is sensible.

Key checks:

- Engine code and gearbox type
- Oil consumption and timing evidence
- S tronic service invoices
- Cooling leaks and thermostat housing condition
- Quattro or Haldex service where applicable

## BMW 3 Series buying logic

The 3 Series can be a sharper and more rewarding used car, but it is sensitive to oil leaks, cooling systems, diesel timing history, and gearbox behavior.

Use the [BMW 3 Series model guide](/models/bmw-3-series) and focus on cold starts, coolant residue, ZF service evidence, and full diagnostic scans.

## Which is the better used buy?

The better car is the one with stronger evidence. A clean A4 beats a neglected 3 Series. A clean 3 Series beats a vague A4. When both histories are equal, choose based on whether you value refinement or driving feel more.
