# Why DSG Service History Matters on Volkswagen

A DSG gearbox can be one of the best parts of a Volkswagen when it is healthy.

But service history matters. Fluid condition, service intervals, driving pattern, and mechatronics behavior can change the risk profile dramatically.

## What to verify

Check:

- DSG type
- Required service interval
- Invoices for fluid/filter service
- Smooth low-speed engagement
- Reverse selection behavior
- Stored gearbox faults
- Shifts under load after warm-up

## Test-drive clues

Pay attention to jerky crawling, delayed reverse, harsh engagement, flared shifts, vibration, or warnings that appear only after heat builds.

## Practical rule

If the DSG service history is missing, treat it as negotiation risk. The gearbox may be fine, but the uncertainty has value.
