# Audi Oil Consumption Questions Before Buying

Oil consumption risk is engine-specific, but buyers should never ignore it.

Before buying a used Audi, identify the exact engine generation and ask direct questions about oil top-ups, service intervals, previous repairs, and warning history.

## Questions to ask

Ask the seller:

- How often does it need oil between services?
- What oil specification is used?
- Are there invoices for oil services?
- Has the PCV system been replaced?
- Were piston/ring or timing repairs ever done?
- Are there low-oil warnings in the history?

## Inspection clues

Look for oil containers in the trunk, smoke after idle, oil residue near intake/PCV areas, and vague answers like "they all use a bit."

Some oil use can be manageable. Unknown oil use is the problem.

## Practical rule

Do not buy an oil-consumption story. Buy documented behavior.
