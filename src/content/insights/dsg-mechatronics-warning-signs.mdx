# DSG Mechatronics Warning Signs Before You Buy

A DSG can feel sharp and efficient when healthy. When neglected, it can become one of the most expensive parts of the buying decision.

## Start with service history

Ask whether the gearbox fluid service applies to that DSG type and whether it was done on time. A seller saying "sealed for life" is not enough for a buyer trying to manage risk.

## Test-drive symptoms

During the drive, pay attention to:

- Harsh engagement from standstill
- Delayed reverse selection
- Jerky low-speed crawling
- Flared shifts under load
- Warning messages after warm-up
- Vibration that changes with gear selection

## Scan before purchase

Fault codes and adaptation data can reveal problems the dashboard does not show. The gearbox may drive acceptably during a short test but still carry stored faults.

## Practical rule

Do not buy a DSG car on "it drives fine" alone. Buy it on service proof, clean diagnostic behavior, and a test drive that includes cold, warm, slow, and loaded conditions.
